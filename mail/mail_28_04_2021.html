APRIL 2021
The Faculty of Dentistry is proud to announce this year’s recipients of the Alumni of Influence award. These awards recognize our outstanding alumni and celebrate the profound ways in which they serve patients, our school, and our communities.

Previously called the Award of Distinction, the Faculty updated these awards in 2020 to better acknowledge dental professionals who influence care in a wider range of ways.

Join us in congratulating Farel Anderson, LouAnn Visconti, and Sneha Abhyankar.

Through their leadership, compassion and tireless commitment, these alumni are championing improved access to care, serving communities in need and building a brighter future — for their patients, the profession and society as a whole.
