Welcome to the Alumni Association
Welcome to a proud and remarkably accomplished international community of over 8,500 U of T Dentistry alumni.

Did you know?

Like every graduate before you, you are automatically enrolled in the Faculty of Dentistry Alumni Association.

As a member of the Faculty of Dentistry Alumni Association (FDAA) and the University of Toronto Alumni Association (UTAA), you now have access to exclusive programming and a variety of learning opportunities, discounts and events.
