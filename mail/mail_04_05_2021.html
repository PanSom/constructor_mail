Induction 2021:
Celebrating the Class of 2T4

On Wednesday, March 3, the Leslie Dan Faculty of Pharmacy hosted its first virtual induction ceremony that welcomed the Doctor of Pharmacy (PharmD) Class of 2T4 to the profession of pharmacy. The ceremony also recognized the beginning of student experiential learning and patient care rotations. Thank you to the Ontario Pharmacists Association, Presenting Sponsor of the Student Support Fund, for providing our students with their professional white coats.

National Pharmacy Awards Recognize LDFP Student and Campaigns

Students at the Leslie Dan Faculty of Pharmacy (LDFP) had an impressive showing in national awards presented by the Canadian Association of Pharmacy Students and Interns (CAPSI), with two students placing third in individual awards and two LDFP health campaigns placing first. Awards were part of the Pharmacy Appreciation Month campaign to celebrate the profession’s adaptability during the pandemic. Thank you to the Ontario Pharmacists Association, Presenting Sponsor of the Student Support Fund, for partnering with CAPSI student leaders to coordinate daily PAM activities.

CPE Speaker Series

The Centre for Practice Excellence hosts a monthly Speaker Series on critical issues impacting the future of health care and pharmacy. The CPE Speaker Series takes place on the first Thursday of each month and usually consist of a 20-30 minute presentation followed by 30 minutes of open discussion and Q&A.

For more information or to be notified of upcoming speakers, please contact the CPE Speaker Series at ups@uoftpharmacy.com
